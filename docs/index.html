Co-parenting conversations, organized

Between keeps shared parenting decisions clear.

Create focused conversations for school, appointments, expenses, handoffs and decisions that should not get lost in a long message thread.

Built for calmer coordination

One conversation per topic, with the context attached.

Between helps separated parents and co-parents keep important messages, files and decisions easier to follow.

Focused topics

Keep each conversation attached to one subject, with clear open, unresolved and resolved states.

Message integrity

Messages cannot be edited or deleted after they are sent, helping every participant keep the same record.

Useful files

Add images, PDFs, spreadsheets and documents so important details stay with the decision.

Calmer replies

Review sensitive messages before sending, then follow unread updates across conversations.

Easy export

Export a conversation and its files into a clear archive that can be shared with a lawyer or used to prepare court material.

Before you send

Improve the message, not the conflict.

When a reply feels sensitive, Between can help you review the wording before it is sent. The goal is to keep the message clear, respectful and focused on the decision to make.

You stay in control of what is sent. The app simply gives you a calmer version to consider before the conversation escalates.
